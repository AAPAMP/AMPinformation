Water System

The water is often turned off between stays. First, turn on any faucet to check — if water flows, you're all set!

If the water is off:

  1. Close the two drain valves in the closet by the front door (near floor, back wall). Turn them so they are perpendicular to the pipes.
  2. Close the yellow valve outside near the bedroom window on the front of the cabin.
  3. Turn the L-shaped metal rod coming out of the ground so it points away from the cabin. This turns the water on.

Good to Know: The cabin uses well water. It's safe for showers and brushing teeth. Bottled water is provided for drinking if preferred.

Golf Cart

The golf cart is old but usable if handled properly. Use only for short trips to the beach and back.

⚠️ Only licensed, insured drivers may operate the cart.

  • Switch under seat: RUN while driving, TOW when not in use
  • If it wasn't charging when you arrived, charge before driving
  • Charge every night
  • Keys on the key rack inside front door (clear "LA" key ring) or in cart
  • Wagon in shed can be pulled manually or towed behind cart

Checkout Checklist

Water System

  • Turn off the main water valve outside (L-shaped pipe to the left of front door)
  • Open the yellow lever next to that valve to drain water from the house
  • Open the two indoor drain valves in the closet near the front door

Laundry

  • Strip the sheets from any beds used
  • Gather all used towels, dish towels, and sheets
  • Deliver to our house in the CWE — leave on front porch, no need to knock!

Doors & Fireplace

  • Lock all doors
  • Kitchen sliding door must be closed firmly before it will lock
  • Living room slider needs to be pulled slightly back from fully closed to engage lock
  • Ensure the fire is completely out and close the fire curtain
  • Restock firewood

Final Items

  • Projector screen rolled back up
  • All lights off
  • Curtains open
  • Outdoor cushions brought inside (don't block vent under stairs)
  • Grill cover replaced
  • Firepit fully extinguished
  • Golf cart plugged in and set to TOW mode
  • Key back in lockbox
