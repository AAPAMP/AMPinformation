Using the Back Gate

The back gate is secured with a Yardlock — a keyless latch. Your Back Gate Code is shown in the Check-In section above.

  1. Enter 9577 on the Yardlock keypad on the outside of the gate.
  2. Once unlocked, pull the lock itself toward you and down to release the latch — the gate will swing open.
  3. After entering, push the gate firmly shut behind you until it clicks locked.

The Yardlock re-locks automatically when the gate closes — just make sure you hear the click.

Ninja Pods & Grounds Coffee Maker

This home has a Ninja Pods & Grounds Specialty Single-Serve Coffee Maker (PB051). It brews K-Cup® compatible pods or ground coffee with 4 brew styles: Classic, Rich, Over Ice, and Specialty.

Brewing with a Pod (K-Cup®)

  1. Check the water reservoir — fill to at least the desired cup size line.
  2. Open the lid and place the Ninja Pod Adapter in the brew basket.
  3. Insert one K-Cup® pod into the adapter.
  4. Close the lid firmly.
  5. Place your mug under the brew nozzle.
  6. Press the Brew Style button to choose: Classic, Rich, Over Ice, or Specialty.
  7. Press the Size button to select your cup size (8, 10, 12, or 24 oz).
  8. Press Start — brewing begins in seconds.

Brewing with Ground Coffee

  1. Check the water reservoir and fill as needed.
  2. Open the lid and place the Permanent Filter in the brew basket.
  3. Add ground coffee using the Ninja Smart Scoop (about 2–3 scoops for 8 oz, 5–7 for 24 oz).
  4. Close the filter and the lid.
  5. Place your mug under the brew nozzle.
  6. Select your brew style and cup size.
  7. Press Start to brew.
Tips: Use Over Ice for iced coffee — it brews a concentrated shot to stay bold over ice. The Specialty setting brews a super-rich 4 oz concentrate, perfect for lattes and cappuccinos with frothed milk. Use only one insert at a time (Pod Adapter or Permanent Filter — never both).

The reservation covers the number of guests listed at booking. If you're expecting daytime visitors, just give us a heads up. This is a duplex and other units may be occupied, so being mindful of your neighbors is always appreciated.

Shared Spaces & Off-Limits Areas

The back yard and back entryway are shared with other residents — please keep these areas tidy. The basement is strictly off-limits to guests.

Children & Outdoor Safety

Children must never be left unattended in the front or back yard. The paved area at the front of the home is not for walking on, and guests should not attempt to interact with, approach, or get close to the pond under any circumstances.

Quiet Hours & No Parties

We're a quiet residential property. Quiet hours are 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM — this includes music, TV, and outdoor conversations. Parties and large gatherings are not permitted.
